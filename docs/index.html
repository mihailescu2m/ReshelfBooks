How it works

Set up your library once — then never forget a book's place again.

📷 Scan to add

Point the camera at a barcode and the title, author, year, and cover are fetched instantly. Assign it to a named shelf like “Living Room Top Row.”

📚 Scan to re-shelve

Pulled a book out? Scan it again and Reshelf Books shows the exact shelf it belongs on. No more wandering or wondering.

🤲 Lending tracker

Lend a book to a friend with a tap. When it comes back, scan it to return it to its shelf.

👨‍👩‍👧 Family sharing

Share your whole library with family over iCloud. Everyone scans, shelves, and returns books together.

Perfect for

Home libraries, classrooms, and anyone whose shelves never stay tidy.

📱 Always-on library assistant

Prop up an old iPad on your bookshelf running full screen — always ready to scan, no unlocking, no navigating.

🏫 Classroom libraries

Catalog the class library, lend books to students by name, and scan each return straight back to the right shelf.

👧 When kids rearrange your shelves

Kids pull books out and rarely put them back right. One scan per stray book turns a frustrating re-sort into a two-second job.

🛒 Never buy duplicates

In a bookshop and not sure if you already own it? Scan the barcode — Reshelf Books tells you, and where it's shelved.

Private by design

Your library lives only in your own iCloud. No accounts to create, nothing to sign up for.
