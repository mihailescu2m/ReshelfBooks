# Book Scanner — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 27 June 2026_

Book Scanner does not collect, store, or share any personal data on our servers.
Your library — books, shelves, covers, and lending records — is stored only in your
own private iCloud account (Apple CloudKit) and synced across your devices. We have
no access to it.

## What the app accesses

- **Camera** — used solely to scan book barcodes on your device.
- **Photo Library** — accessed only when you choose to set a cover image.
- **Book lookups** — to fetch a book's details, the app sends only the scanned ISBN
  (or the title/author you type) to public book-data services: Open Library, Google
  Books, Crossref, the Library of Congress, Trove, Inventaire, Apple Books (iTunes),
  WorldCat, Bookcover API, and Better World Books. No personal information is
  transmitted.
- **Family Sharing** — sharing a library uses Apple's CloudKit sharing; shared data
  is handled by Apple between the participants you invite.

## What the app does NOT do

- No analytics.
- No tracking.
- No third-party advertising SDKs.
- No developer-operated servers or accounts.
